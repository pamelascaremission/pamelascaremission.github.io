Mission

Founded on community, sustained by community.

Pamela's Care Mission was born from a desire to create purpose and connection for adults with disabilities. What began as a small effort to empower individuals soon grew into a network where disabled adults provide meaningful services to veterans in need. Over time, it has evolved into a peer-to-peer community that bridges the gap of loneliness and isolation often experienced by those living with disabilities or in challenging circumstances.
